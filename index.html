HONEYDEW VIEW

A game about life, romance and a taste of the paranormal.
Honeydew View is a Visual Novel about navigating high school, focusing on deep emotional connections and telling a story that reflects who the player is, and what they do within the game.

LEARN MORE WHERE TO PLAY

Live, Love and Learn

Honeydew View is meant to create an experience that can be played the way that you want to play it. There is an emphasis on letting the player drive the story and decide what they want to do next. 

Play as Yourself

Honeydew View has been designed to provide the framework for all players to enjoy expressing themselves, no matter their race, gender or sexuality.

Love is Love 

Our game has a series of romancable options of many genders, sexualities, races or even species! Don't feel limited, and go out on dates with whomever you'd like.

Branching Narrative

Your actions will effect your story. Certain types of actions might even change a character's opinions of your personality, or your reputation as a whole.

Amazing Artwork

The team has worked with quite a few talented artists in order to bring you a game that's visual beauty can help tell this exciting story with you!
